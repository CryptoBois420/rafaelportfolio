About Me

Hi, I'm Orion! I'm a 16-year-old artist from France. I've been drawing and painting for as long as I can remember, and I'm passionate about creating art that tells stories. I'm currently working on a webcomic called "Solar's End". It's a fantasy adventure story about a young boy who sets out on a quest to save humanity. I love to experiment with different art styles, and I'm always looking for new ways to challenge myself. I'm also a big fan of anime and manga, and my art is often influenced by those genres. I'm excited to share my work with the world, and I hope you'll enjoy it! Here are some of my favorite things about art: The ability to express myself creatively. The challenge of coming up with new ideas. The satisfaction of seeing my work come to life. The opportunity to connect with other artists and fans.
